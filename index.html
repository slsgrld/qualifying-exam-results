PLACEMENT PROTOCOL

A major is considered eligible only if the student earns at least 15 points in its Scientific Aptitude section.

For each eligible major, a weighted total score is computed using the formula:

(Aptitude ÷ 30 × 60) + (Interest ÷ 30 × 20) + (Situational ÷ 30 × 20)

The major with the highest weighted total becomes the assigned track.

If no major reaches the 15-point threshold in Aptitude, the fallback rule applies: assign the major with the highest total score regardless of eligibility. If the highest scoring major is already full, placement proceeds to the next eligible major with available slots based on ranking.
